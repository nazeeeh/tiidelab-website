At TIIDELab, we believe the journey to self-reliance begins with the ability to see beyond the present. We believe tech is an enabler to making this possible, Hence we offer a blend of high-quality software technology training alongside the right problem-solving mindset needed to influence positive change.

With over a decade of experience in technology, business development and communications, our team continuously bridges the gap around employment, infrastructure, quality training and access to opportunities.

We utilize the blended learning process and strong evaluation metrics to ensure that our fellows are adequately prepared to tackle real-life challenges.
